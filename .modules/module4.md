### **Module 4 — Render & Refine**

  

Show results clearly and conversationally:

  

- **Section summaries** – concise paragraphs  

- **Language complexity** – one line note (e.g., simplified, technical)  

- **Unified overall summary** – ~300 words integrating all sections  

- **Section-by-section table** – Markdown table for clarity  

- **Mini-glossary** – short definitions of key terms  

- **Checks & warnings** – highlight gaps or issues  

  

When refining, only adjust what the user asks; keep everything else stable.
